Zero Bloat

Over time, many companies seemed to have forgotten that not everyone has a fast internet connection. High resolution images and bloated frameworks have become the norm. But here at Codon64, we recognize that all data comes with a cost.

For that reason, we have made the decision to ensure that every page on this site has a weight of 10 kb or less. Which is to say that each page can be loaded by your browser with 10kb or less of data transfer. To meet this goal, we have:

  • Zero cookies or tracking of any kind
  • Zero frameworks or external libraries
  • Zero raster images - Our favicon is an SVG

We do have JavaScript, but it would be impossible to do the encoding and decoding operations without it

Zero AI

Codon64 is a grass-feed, free-range, USDA certified lean website. Made by me, a human; not a glorified text prediction algorithm that poisons our minds and bodies.
